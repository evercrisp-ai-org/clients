One question every surgeon practice owner should ask their CPA this quarter:

"When was the last time we recalculated my S-Corp salary against my retirement plan capacity?"

Because here's the thing: your S-Corp salary isn't just a payroll number. It determines how much you can contribute to your 401(k), your cash balance plan, and how your QBI deduction is calculated.

A number that was right three years ago is almost certainly wrong today. Contribution limits changed. Tax law changed. Your income probably changed too.

One conversation. One recalculation. Potentially $15K–$25K in additional wealth-building capacity per year.

That's a good use of 30 minutes.
