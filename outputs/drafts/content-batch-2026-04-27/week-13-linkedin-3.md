$300K+ in personal goodwill savings, $1.1M in valuation clarity, and one question that matters more than all of it: what are you transitioning to?

This quarter we covered the mechanics. PE deal structures and where the headline number hides complexity. Personal goodwill and the tax strategy hidden inside your reputation. Practice valuations and the three lines that move the real outcome. Five-year exit timelines and the documentation that takes years to build.

All of that matters. Getting the deal right, the tax treatment right, and the valuation right can mean hundreds of thousands of dollars in preserved wealth.

But here is the insight underneath all of it. The surgeons who are happiest two years after a transition are the ones who knew what their money was for before it arrived. They did the identity work. They built purpose architecture. They put financial guardrails in place. They planned the next chapter with the same intentionality they brought to every surgical plan.

The deal determines how much wealth you create. What you do with it determines whether it lasts.

What is your next chapter plan?
