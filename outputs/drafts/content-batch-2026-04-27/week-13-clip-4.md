## Content

### Caption Script

Retirement is a financial construct. It means you have enough money to stop working.

Transition is a human construct. It means you have a plan for who you become next.

The surgeons who thrive after the OR are the ones who knew what their money was for. They planned the next chapter with the same precision they brought to every surgical case. They did the identity work. They built purpose architecture. They put financial guardrails in place.

They didn't just exit. They transitioned. Into purpose, into contribution, into a life they designed rather than one they defaulted into.

Stop planning your retirement. Start planning your transition.
