## On-camera script (~60 seconds)

"$330,000 of a four-million-dollar practice sale is hiding in the tax treatment of your personal goodwill. Personal goodwill, your reputation, your referral network, gets taxed at 23.8% when documented. Without documentation, the same value can be taxed above 40%.

The IRS requires this documentation to exist before any sale process begins. Two years minimum. Courts have cut 75% allocations to 15% when the paperwork showed up with the deal.

Three items to put in motion this quarter if you're within five years of any practice transition: a non-compete agreement between you and your entity, a systematic referral tracking process, and a calendar note to commission a formal goodwill appraisal in 18 months.

The cost of starting is a few thousand dollars. The cost of not starting is measured in hundreds of thousands."
