$87,500 over five years for money that felt safe.

A low-yield savings balance can feel safe while hiding an unpriced cost. The better question is whether each dollar has a time horizon attached to it.

Here is the diagnostic lens: the decision has to match the right time horizon, tax consequence, and practice reality. At surgeon income levels, a small structural mismatch can become a large annual leak.

Can you name which dollars you need in 30 days, 12 months, and 3 years?
