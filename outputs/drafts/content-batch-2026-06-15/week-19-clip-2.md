### Caption Script

$36,000 or $18,000. By July, that gap quietly decides your December.

The 2026 defined contribution limit is $72,000. If you intend to max it, you should be near halfway by now, around $36,000.

Plenty of surgeons are sitting at $18,000, because the 401(k) deferral has been pacing but the employer and profit-sharing pieces have not.

Here is why it matters: you cannot fix that with a December wish. Payroll has to actually run those contributions across your remaining paychecks. Caught now, it is a schedule change. Caught in December, it is a contribution you legally cannot make.

Where are you against $36,000 today?
