## Content

### Caption Script

The fall financial review feels like the responsible move. Here is the catch.

By the time fall arrives, most of your year is already written. Payroll cannot reasonably add a large retirement contribution. The overhead drift already happened. Your income is what it is.

A midyear check is different. You still have real data and real time to act on it. It is the same idea as confirming the vitals partway through a long case, catching what drifted while the correction still matters.

Block one Saturday this summer. Your December self will thank you.
