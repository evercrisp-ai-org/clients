## Content

### Caption Script

Whether you should pull income forward or push it out comes down to four questions.

One: is a practice sale on the calendar? A sale year is heavy, so you usually want income out of it.

Two: is there a real low-income gap year coming, a sabbatical or a wind-down? That is when deferral genuinely pays.

Three: are you changing states? A move to a no-tax state changes the value of every deferred dollar.

Four: how is your retirement funding paced? Income timing feeds the contributions that shelter the most.

Answer those four honestly and the direction usually picks itself.
