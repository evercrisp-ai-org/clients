Imagine a surgeon looking at the same $200,000 of income three different years in a row and getting a different answer each time about when to take it. One year it should sit tight, the next it should move into a quieter year ahead, the year after it should be pulled forward before a sale. The dollars never changed. What changed was the year on the other side of the decision. Income timing only works when you actually know what next year looks like.
