Quick question for plan owners: is your cash balance plan funded to a schedule, or just to the calendar? A 401(k) forgives a slow start. A cash balance plan does not, because it is a defined benefit plan that expects steady funding all year. Fifteen minutes with your target, the mid-year mark, and your funded-to-date tells you everything you need to know about whether the second half is going to be smooth or stressful.
