## Content

### Caption Script

Conference season is here. Before you sit down with any PE firm, ask these three questions.

Question one: What was the timeline from first conversation to close for your most recent deal? Clean, well-structured deals take 9 to 14 months. If someone closed in four months, the surgeon probably didn't have time to optimize their tax structure or document personal goodwill.

Question two: How was personal goodwill handled? If the acquirer looks confused, that tells you something. Personal goodwill allocation is a core deal term that determines whether your proceeds are taxed at 23.8% or 40.8%. It should be negotiated, not treated as a tax footnote.

Question three: What does the earnout structure look like three years after close? The headline multiple means nothing if 40% of the price is contingent on metrics you can't control.

Timeline tells you the pace. Goodwill tells you the sophistication. Earnout tells you the risk. Those three answers tell you everything.
