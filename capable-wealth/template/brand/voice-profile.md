### Never Use Em Dashes
Em dashes are a common default in AI-generated text. Unless the voice owner explicitly and frequently uses em dashes in their sample content, ban them. Use commas, parentheses, periods, semicolons, or colons instead.

### Never Use the "It's Not X. It's Y." Pivot Construction
The rhetorical pattern of "It's not X. It's Y." (and its variations) reads as formulaic and manufactured. Contrarian reframes should be woven into the flow of the argument, not delivered as blunt two-sentence pivots.
