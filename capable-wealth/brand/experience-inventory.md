## Usage Rules for Content Creation

1. **Before drafting any content**, consult this inventory to determine what story classifications are available for the topic.
2. **Every client story** must trace to a specific entry in Section 4 (Verified Stories) to use `[REAL-ANONYMIZED]` classification.
3. **Experience-based framing** ("I've seen this," "in my practice") is only permitted for topics listed under Section 5, Practiced.
4. **Knowledge-based framing** ("as a CFP," "the planning opportunity is") should be used for topics listed under Section 5, Studied.
5. **If a topic has no entry** in this inventory, default to `[GENERAL-PRINCIPLE]` classification and frame the content around the financial concept itself, not around personal experience delivering it.
6. **Section 8 is absolute.** If something is listed as off-limits, no amount of anonymization or framing makes it acceptable.
7. **Update this document** whenever new client engagements, outcomes, or credentials are established. Content accuracy depends on this inventory being current.
