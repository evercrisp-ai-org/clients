### Caption Script

When you sell your practice, the IRS asks one question: how much of this was the surgeon, and how much was the business?

Your answer, backed by documentation, could mean the difference between a $950,000 tax bill and a $1,280,000 tax bill. That's $330,000.

The key is something called personal goodwill. Your reputation, your referral network, your patient relationships. That value, properly documented, gets taxed at 23.8%. Without documentation, it could be taxed at over 40%.

The catch? The documentation must be in place years before the sale. Start now or lose the window.
