## Content

$121,600 in tax savings — from donating stock to a charitable fund instead of cash.

The math is well-known among high-income professionals. What is less known: the account takes six to eight weeks to establish.

Most surgeons plan to set it up in November. By then, the transfer clears in January. The 2026 deduction moves to 2027.

The most powerful year-end tax moves for practice owners are not complicated. They just have earlier deadlines than people expect. Donor-advised fund setup, retirement plan amendments, equipment purchase timing — all of them require initiation in August or September to land in the current tax year.

If you have been thinking "I'll get to that before year-end," July is when that starts. Not October.
