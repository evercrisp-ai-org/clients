## Content

### Caption Script

Can a retirement plan actually fail? A cash balance plan can.

Here is what chronic underfunding really costs. First, the deduction you built the plan to capture shrinks, so your tax bill goes back up. Second, a funding deficiency can carry an excise tax.

Third, and this is the one nobody mentions, a plan that keeps missing its required contributions can lose its qualified status. That unwinds years of tax-deferred treatment in a single stroke.

And almost none of that comes from anything reckless. It comes from treating a year-long obligation like a December errand. Confirm your funding now, while it is still easy to fix.
