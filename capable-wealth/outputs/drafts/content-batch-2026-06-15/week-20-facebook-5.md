Most retirement accounts forgive a slow year. Your cash balance plan does not. Chronic underfunding can shrink the deduction you built it for, trigger an excise tax on the shortfall, and in the worst case cost the plan its qualified status. The reassuring part is that none of that happens by surprise. It happens from late attention, and a fifteen-minute check this month is how you keep it from ever starting.
