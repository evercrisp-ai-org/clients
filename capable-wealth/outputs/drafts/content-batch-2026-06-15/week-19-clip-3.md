## Content

### Caption Script

Five numbers tell you whether your financial year is actually on track.

One: income versus your projection. Annualize what you have earned so far.

Two: tax pacing. Taxes paid against your projected bill, now that the Q2 estimate is behind you.

Three: retirement progress. Where you stand against the $72,000 contribution limit.

Four: practice overhead. Your ratio versus last year and your benchmark.

Five: personal spending rate. The real share of after-tax income leaving the household.

Put each one next to last year, give each one decision, and you have a halftime report that fits on a single page.
